Management

The management structure of NESADI SACCO is consistent with the law, By-Laws and the best recommended management practices in the SACCOs subsector nationally and internationally.

We aspire to further perfect the good management practices so as to serve our members in the best ways possible. As expected and is the norm, the top decision making organ of NESADI SACCO is the Annual or Special General Meeting of members followed by the Board of Directors (BOD) and then the day-to-day management team of the SACCO led by the Chairman and advised by the Patron. The management of NESADI SACCO as currently constituted comprises of the following positions and their current holders;
